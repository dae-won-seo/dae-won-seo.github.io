Hello! I am an assistant professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (previously ICE) at DGIST.

My main research theme is to improve information processing systems, such as data communication, data storage, and machine learning from a theoretical standpoint. Main analysis tools are from information theory, probability, statistics, and queueing theory. Please see the publication list and group website for details.
