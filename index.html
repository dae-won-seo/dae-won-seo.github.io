Hello! I am an assistant professor in the Department of Information and Communication Engineering at DGIST.

My main research theme is to understand how learning systems work. In particular, I am interested in how deep neural networks work and learning over social networks takes place. Main analysis tools are from probability, statistics, information theory, and queueing theory. Please see the publication list for details.
